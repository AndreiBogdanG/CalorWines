Our Story

Based in Cyprus, our company is dedicated to introducing the richness and authenticity of premium Romanian wines to the local market. We carefully select high-quality wines from renowned Romanian wineries, working closely with passionate producers who combine tradition, craftsmanship, and modern winemaking techniques.

Romania has a long and distinguished viticultural history, with diverse terroirs and native grape varieties that create wines of remarkable character and elegance. Our mission is to bring these unique flavors to Cyprus, offering customers an opportunity to discover exceptional red, white, and rosé wines that reflect both heritage and innovation.

We focus on quality, authenticity, and reliability in every bottle we import. From boutique family wineries to internationally awarded producers, our portfolio is curated to meet the expectations of wine enthusiasts, restaurants, hotels, and retail partners seeking distinctive and premium selections.

Through strong partnerships, professional service, and a deep appreciation for wine culture, we aim to build a bridge between Romania’s celebrated vineyards and Cyprus’ vibrant wine community.
